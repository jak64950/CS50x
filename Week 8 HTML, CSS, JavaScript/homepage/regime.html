Pricing

Our training will take someone with no dive experience to a fully certified Master Diver. Our training will consists of at least 50 dives under various conditions going to a depth of up to 130ft. You will learn safety protocols, rescue, night, deep, navigation, and cavern diving at a minimum. We will not only do typical diving, we will also be performing tasks underwater to simulate tasks you may perform while in space. This journey will be physically and mentally draining, but it will be the most fun you've ever had.














Be prepared to learn how to fly a T-38 training jet. We will take you from no flying experience to earning a full Commercial Pilot's license. Our training consists of over 50 hours of training flight and over 250 hours of in pilot flight time. You will be tested with a variety of casualty drills and must learn to work with our team to overcome each challenge. This is the part where you develop your leadership, problem solving, and stress control skills. All of these are mandatory if you want to go to space.














The last part of our training is language tutoring. We offer expert tutors in over 12 languages to get you from absolute beginner to working fluency. The language you learn will depend on your goals/interests and what agency you are trying to apply for as an astronaut. We can teach up to three languages at once if you have a knack for it, but if any of your other training suffers, we will limit it as needed.
